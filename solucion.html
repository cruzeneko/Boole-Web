Welcome to Boole-Web!

Boole-Web is a simple digital electronics and design educational tool developed at the University of Deusto that will (hopefully) replace the outdated Boole-Deusto tool.

We hope that you are productive using this tool and ask you to report any issues you may find to eneko.cruz@opendeusto.es, luis.rodriguezgil@deusto.es or zubia@deusto.es.
